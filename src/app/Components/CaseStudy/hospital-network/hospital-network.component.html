UAE Premier Hospital Network: $1.8M Recovered, 5 Locations Standardized

The Crisis

A multi-site network across Abu Dhabi and Dubai was leaking ~$2.3M annually—coding inconsistencies, denial cascades, fragmented processes, and below-benchmark patient satisfaction. Expansion was on hold.

What We Did

  • Built a centralized RCM/ops command center.
  • Standardized compliant coding and documentation protocols system wide.
  • Launched patient journey improvements (access, reminders, follow-ups)
  • Trained leadership on operational governance and KPIs

The Results (12 months)

  • $1.8M recovered revenue (≈78% leakage eliminated)
  • 95% net collection rate across sites
  • Patient satisfaction +35 points
  • Expanded to 2 new cities without quality slippage.
  • Operational costs −25% via standardization
